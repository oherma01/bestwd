About Me

Founded in 2013, Best Web Design is the product of a lifetime love a programming, design and business.

Best Web Design was founded and is run by me, Oliver Herman. My goal is to be a high quality resource for anyone in need of flexible, low-cost web sites in the eastern Massachusetts area and beyond. There has always been great need online for competent designers and developers, and the business has grown greatly since I started tinkering around with websites some eight years ago. That said, I'm very familiar with the creative process and I'm prepared to work on projects large and small, from graphic design to server-side programming. If you have any questions, you can contact me via email at oherman@bestwd.net or my cell (978-879-6677). All prices will be negotiated up front.
